CS 180 Project 5: Fun with Diffusion Models!

In this project, we use a pre-trained diffusion model (DeepFloyd IF) to perform a variety of image generation and modification tasks. Then, we train our own diffusion model architecture from scratch on the MNIST digit dataset.

CS 180 Project 4: Neural Radiance Fields!

In this project, we perform the camera calibration and develop the neural network architecture to train our own neural radiance fields.

CS 180 Project 3: (Auto)Stitching and Photo Mosaics

In this project, we use homographic transformations and image blending techniques to stitch images into photo mosaics (i.e. panoramas).

CS 180 Project 2: Fun with Filters and Frequencies!

In this project, we use convolutional filters and their relationship with the image frequency domain to perform a variety of interesting image processing applications, including image blurring, edge detection, image sharpening, hybrid images, and image blending.

CS 180 Project 1: Images of the Russian Empire -- Colorizing the Prokudin-Gorskii Photo Collection
